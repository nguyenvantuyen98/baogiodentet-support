Frequently asked questions

How do I open the customization menu?

Please long-press on the countdown screen. The customization menu will slide up from the bottom.

Why does tapping the screen not show the menu?

This screen uses long-press, not single tap. Hold your finger for about 0.5-1 second.

Where can I change the background?
  • Long-press on the screen.
  • Select Change background.
  • Swipe left/right to preview templates.
  • Tap Save.
What does "Choose format" mean?

It controls how remaining time is displayed. You can choose:

  • Remaining X days ...
  • Remaining X weeks Y days ...
  • Remaining X months Y days ...
Why does the "month" value sometimes differ from calendar months?

In the app, month format currently uses a fixed 30-days-per-month rule for faster display. So it may differ slightly from real calendar months.

How do I turn hour and minute display on/off?

Long-press on the screen, then toggle Show hour, minute in the menu.

Is the blinking colon between hour and minute a bug?

No. This is an intentional visual effect synchronized with time rhythm.

How do I change font style and size?

Long-press on the screen, choose Font style or Font size, swipe to preview, then tap Save.

I finished swiping options. How do I apply changes?

Tap Save (top-right corner). If you tap Cancel, current settings remain unchanged.

Will my customization settings be lost after closing the app?

No. Customizations (background, style, format, font size, hour-minute) are saved locally and kept when reopening the app.

What date does the app count down to?

The app counts down to the next Lunar New Year date from the built-in Tet date list.

Do I need internet for the app to work?

Usually no internet is required for core countdown features, because the app uses system time and built-in date data.

How do I add a widget to iPhone home screen? (iOS)
  • Long-press an empty area on the Home Screen.
  • Select Edit then Add Widget.
  • Find the app.
  • Choose a widget style and tap Add Widget.
  • Tap Done to finish.
How do I add a widget on Android home screen?
  • Long-press an empty area on the home screen.
  • Select Widgets.
  • Find the app.
  • Long-press the widget and drag it to your desired position.
How do I remove a widget?
  • iOS: Long-press the widget, then select Remove Widget.
  • Android: Long-press the widget and drag it to Remove/Delete area (depends on device brand).
Can I resize widgets?
  • Android: most launchers allow resize handles.
  • iOS: usually you need to re-add the widget using a different size.
Why does the widget not update every second?

This is a normal operating system limitation to save battery and resources. Widgets usually refresh on system schedule, not real-time like the main app screen.

Note: widget refresh frequency can vary by device model and OS version.
