Frequently asked questions

Where do I open countdown settings?

Tap the screen to show the gear button, then tap that button to open the settings page.

Why does the gear button disappear?

The button auto-hides after about 3 seconds. Just tap the screen again to show it.

How do I change the background?
  • Open the Background tab.
  • Swipe left/right or tap the left/right arrows to switch templates.
Where can I change countdown display format?

Open the Count Style tab and choose:

  • By day
  • By week + day
  • By month + day
Why can the “month” value differ from calendar intuition?

Because in code, month is currently converted as 30 days per month for quick display.

How do I change text effects?

Open the Text Effects tab and swipe or tap arrows to choose a style.

Where do I adjust font size?

Open the Font Size tab and drag the slider. Current range is 0.5 to 2.0.

Where do I adjust countdown line position?

Open the Text Position tab and drag the slider. You can move text up or down as needed.

How do I turn hour & minute display on/off?

Open the Hour & Minute tab to switch between on and off.

Why is the colon between hour and minute blinking?

This is an intentional effect synchronized with each second.

Where do I change app language?

Open the Language tab and choose Tiếng Việt or English.

Are settings auto-saved?

Yes, with the new mechanism:

  • When you switch tabs: previous tab is saved (if there are changes).
  • Revert button: restores current tab to its saved state.
  • Done button: saves current tab and exits.
  • System Back button: discards unsaved changes of current tab and exits.
When does the app show “Happy New Year!”?

When remaining days <= 0. If hour/minute is enabled, it shows 00h : 00p (or 00h : 00m in English).

Does the app need internet to run countdown?

Internet is not required for core countdown logic; the app uses built-in Tet date data and device time.

How do I add widgets on iOS?

iOS: go to Home Screen, long-press background until icons jiggle, choose Edit → Add Widget → select widget → Add Widget → Done.

How do I add widgets on Android?

Android: long-press empty Home Screen area → Widgets → choose app → long-press widget and drag to screen.

How do I resize/remove widgets?
  • Android: long-press widget to resize, or drag to Remove area to delete.
  • iOS: long-press widget to edit/rearrange; remove widget from context menu.
Why does the widget not update in real-time every second?

Widgets are always limited by operating system policies:

  • Android: updatePeriodMillis does not support less than 30 minutes (for periodic updates).
  • iOS: WidgetKit uses timeline/scheduling; extension does not run continuously like the app screen.
Where do I open system App Settings?
  • iOS: Settings → Apps → choose the app.
  • Android: Settings → Apps → choose the app → Permissions.
